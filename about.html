Meet Jeri

My background is in traditional graphic design, but I found my true calling in making complex things efficient. I'm a firm believer that both a good day and a good design should be simple and free of noise — it's a "less is more" approach that has followed me from the tech scenes of Austin and Seattle to my current home in Amsterdam, my favorite city in the world.

When I'm not at my desk, you can usually find me traveling; I love the perspective that comes with a bit of "culture shock" and the challenge of navigating unfamiliar places. When I'm keeping things local, I'm usually working on a bookbinding project, heading to a comedy club, or burning off energy on the padel court and training for a half marathon. I'm always looking for a team that values transparency and trust — one that prioritizes the kind of rapport where big ideas and bad jokes are equally welcome.

Maren and Berne's Birthday Wish

Maren and Berne's first birthday was fast approaching and their parents wanted to do something a little different. The twins were born with Cystic Fibrosis and it was their parents dream to one day find a cure. The birthday card helped to educate people on Cystic Fibrosis and what we can do to help.
